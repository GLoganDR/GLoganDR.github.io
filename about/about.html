Education

Brief Background

I was born and raised in Illinois in the middle of the state surrounded by farmland and a mid-sized forest. I didn't get my first computer until the age of 14 in 2001. I quickly learned how to take care of software/hardware issues, and I became more and more "techy" as time went on. My family wanted me to go into Development then, but I was too nervous to do it. For 6 years, I was a vocalist for a couple of touring acts. I decided to hang up the mic for a career recently. I took a year of C++ in computer sciences class in high school, and my interest was piqued then. I recently decided to follow through with Development because I wanted to feel like I was proud of my work. I love the feeling of seeing my code come to life and watching something build on itself.
